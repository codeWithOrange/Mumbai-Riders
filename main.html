About Mumbai Riders

Mumbai Riders is a premier bike store dedicated to serving motorcycle enthusiasts in Mumbai. Offering a wide range of motorcycles, from cruisers to sportbikes, the store features an extensive selection of popular brands and models. With a knowledgeable and friendly staff, Mumbai Riders provides personalized assistance to help customers find the perfect bike. The store also offers a variety of motorcycle accessories, gear, and services, ensuring a comprehensive experience for riders of all levels. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rider, Mumbai Riders is the go-to destination for all your biking needs

Kawasaki Ninja

The Kawasaki Ninja series is a renowned line of sport motorcycles known for their sleek design, powerful engines, and high-performance capabilities. Models in the series range from entry-level bikes like the Ninja 250, priced around ₹3 lakh, to high-performance options like the Ninja ZX-10R, which can cost approximately ₹16 lakh. The supercharged Ninja H2 is among the most expensive, with prices starting around ₹35 lakh. With aerodynamic fairings, aggressive aesthetics, and advanced technology, the Ninja series has established itself as a favorite among sportbike enthusiasts and professional racers alike.

price:₹3-35 lakh

The KTM 1290 Super Duke R

The KTM 1290 Super Duke R is a powerful naked motorcycle known for its aggressive styling and exceptional performance. Powered by a 1301cc V-twin engine, it delivers impressive torque and horsepower, making it one of the most exhilarating bikes in the naked bike segment. The bike features advanced electronics, including ride modes, traction control, and cornering ABS, along with a lightweight chassis for agile handling. With a bold design and a sporty riding position, the Super Duke R is designed for those seeking a thrilling ride on both the street and track.

price:₹19-22 lakh

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-14

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-14 is a powerful sportbike known for its impressive performance and high-speed capabilities. It features a robust 1441cc inline-four engine, delivering exceptional horsepower and torque, making it one of the fastest motorcycles on the market. The bike boasts an aerodynamic design for enhanced stability at high speeds, advanced electronics like traction control and power modes, and comfortable ergonomics for both long rides and spirited performance.

price:₹18-22 lakh

The Kawasaki Z750

The Kawasaki Z750 is a versatile naked motorcycle known for its blend of performance, comfort, and style. It features a 748cc inline-four engine that offers a balance of power and manageable torque, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced riders. With its aggressive styling and minimalistic design, the Z750 stands out on the road while providing a comfortable riding position for city commuting and longer journeys alike. The bike's lightweight chassis contributes to agile handling, making it a favorite among those who enjoy spirited rides

price:₹400

The Honda Wave series

The Honda Wave series is a popular line of underbone motorcycles known for their reliability, efficiency, and practicality. Powered by a 110cc single-cylinder engine, the Wave offers a smooth ride with good fuel economy, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting. Its lightweight design and agile handling make it easy to navigate through urban traffic. The series features a minimalist design, comfortable seating, and a range of modern amenities, such as electric start and ample storage options.

price:₹70,000-90,000

BMW S1000RR

The BMW S1000RR is a high-performance sportbike known for its cutting-edge technology and exceptional speed. Powered by a 999cc inline-four engine, it delivers impressive horsepower and torque, making it one of the top contenders in the superbike category. The bike features advanced electronics, including traction control, multiple riding modes, and cornering ABS, enhancing both performance and safety. Its aerodynamic design, with aggressive lines and a lightweight frame, contributes to outstanding handling and stability at high speeds

price:₹20-24 lakh
